# French 75

## Ingredients

- 50ml Dry Gin
- 2 barspoons Simple syrup
- 20ml lemon juice
- 80ml brut champagne
- Lemon peel

## Instructions

- Glassware: champagne glass or highball
- Garnish: Lemon zest twist

Shake gin, simple syrup and lemon juice in shaker with ice. Pour into glass and top with champagne.
