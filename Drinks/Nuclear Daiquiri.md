# Nuclear Daiquiri

## Ingredients

* **30 ml** overproof rum ( *Wray & Nephew* )
* **20 ml** *Chartreuse Verte*
* **30 ml** *Falernum #8* or *Velvet Falernum*
* **30 ml** lime juice

## Instructions

Shake all ingredients with ice and fine strain into chilled glass.
