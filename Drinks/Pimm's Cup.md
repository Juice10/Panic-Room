# Pimm's Cup

## Ingredients

- 2 oz Pimm's no 1.
- .5 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
- 3 oz Ginger Ale
- Mint sprig
- Lemon wheel
- Cucumber Slice

## Instructions

Build the drink in a highball glass, first Pimm's and lemon juice, then ice, then ginger ale. Stir gently, garnish with the mint sprig, cucumber and lemon wheel, and serve.
