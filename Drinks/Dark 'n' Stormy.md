# Dark ’n’ Stormy

## Ingredients

- 60ml Dark Rum
- 15ml Fresh lime juice
- 90ml Chilled ginger beer
- 1 lime wedge

## Instructions

Glassware: Highball
Ice: Cubes in glass
Garnish: Lime wedge

Build the drink in a highball, first rum and lime juice, then ice, then ginger beer. Stir gently, garnish with the lime wedge, and serve.
