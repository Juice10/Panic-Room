# Gin & Tonic

## Ingredients

- 60ml Gin
- 15ml Fresh lime juice
- 90ml Chilled Tonic
- 1 lime wedge

## Instructions

- Glassware: any
- Ice: Cubes in glass
- Garnish: Lime wedge

Build the drink in a glass, first gin and lime juice, then ice, then tonic. Stir gently, garnish with the lime wedge, and serve.
