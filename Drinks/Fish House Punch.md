# Fish House Punch

## Ingredients

- 30ml Cognac
- 30ml Dark Rum
- 20ml Peach Liqueur
- 20ml Lemon Juice
- 1 barspoon simple syrup
- 45ml tea (cold black english breakfast tea)

## Instructions

- Glassware: Highball
- Ice: Cubes in glass
- Garnish: Lemon wheel

Add all the ingredients to a shaker with ice. Shake, then strain into a chilled highball glass over ice. Garnish with a lemon slice.
